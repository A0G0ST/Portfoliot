About Me

August Dahlström

Hi! My name is August, and I'm a front-end developer with a degree in web development. I have a strong passion for creating interactive, responsive, and user-friendly web experiences. I also have a big interest in design

During my studies, I specialized in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern front-end frameworks like React. I also studied back-end development, which has given me a broad understanding of the entire development process from database management and APIs to server logic and full-stack solutions.

My passion lies in building beautiful and functional web applications where design and performance go hand in hand. I always strive to write clean, scalable, and optimized code while trying to stay up to date with the latest technologies in web development.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy gaming, good food and spending time with friends and family.
